# Talan — AI Engineering Lead — Salary Target

**Proposal received:** 150k CHF base + 30k CHF max bonus (180k CHF OTE max)
**Date drafted:** 2026-05-01

---

## Bottom line

**150k base + 30k bonus (180k OTE max) is below market for this role.** It's particularly underwhelming given (a) the scope they describe, (b) my seniority, and (c) Geneva CoL.

---

## Why the JD vs. salary feels misaligned

**The role they're describing is Director-level, not Senior IC level:**

- They explicitly want "Tech Lead, Architecte ou CTO" background
- 50% delivery includes "directeur technique" responsibilities and direct CIO advisory
- 30% GTM means I'd personally sell — flash advisory, audits, coach craft missions
- 20% enablement means building a coreteam IA across 170 people in Switzerland
- "Thought Leader" positioning on the Romandie market with Trust Valley / EPFL visibility

---

## Geneva benchmarks

For consulting + AI/agentic specialty + Swiss finance domain:

| Profile | Base | Bonus / variable |
|---|---|---|
| Big 4 / mid-tier consulting Senior Manager / Director | ~180–220k | 30–50k |
| Senior IC AI/ML lead in regulated finance | ~160–200k | 15–30k |
| CTO / Field CTO with sovereign-cloud expertise | ~200–280k | variable + LTI |
| Consulting roles with explicit GTM | — | 25–40% variable, sometimes commission |

**Their offer (150k base / 20% bonus cap) sits closer to a Senior Tech Lead band than a Director / Lead Évangéliste band.**

---

## Specific structural problems with the package

1. **Bonus structure doesn't reward GTM.** They're asking for 30% of my time on commercial work but capping variable at 20%. In consulting, when there's explicit sales responsibility, the comp typically includes commission on closed deals or a higher target bonus. Otherwise the incentive is off.

2. **No mention of equity / partner track / value-share.** The JD says *"Package aligné sur la création de valeur"* — that phrase usually implies upside (carried interest, partnership track, deal commission). A flat 180k cap is the opposite of value-aligned.

3. **Step-down vs. my trajectory.** My last three signals (Field CTO Wonderful, Director SE Ripple managing ~50 engineers across two continents, Global Head of Tech FlowBank running a 40-person FINMA-regulated IT org) all sit in 200k+ territory in Geneva. Accepting 150k anchors me below my peer band.

4. **Their own positioning works against the number.** They want a "thought leader on the Romandie place" who anchors sovereignty narratives at Trust Valley/EPFL. That's a senior commercial profile — not paid like a delivery lead.

---

## What would feel right

For the role they wrote, a market-aligned package looks more like:

- **Base:** 180–200k CHF
- **Target bonus:** 30–50k (20–25% target, with measured upside)
- **Plus** a commission/origination component on net new mandates I bring or close
- **Or** explicit partner-track visibility within 18–24 months

---

## Nuances before pushing back

- **Talan is mid-tier consulting, not Big 4** — they don't have McKinsey/EY pricing power, so absolute numbers are a notch lower than tier-1 consulting benchmarks.
- **Greenfield offer** — they're asking me to build the AI offer from scratch in Switzerland. That's leverage on my side (they need a builder, not a backfill), but also means there's not yet a P&L track record they can point to when defending top-of-band comp.
- **The "no team management" framing cuts both ways.** It's a deliberately IC-positioned role — which can justify slightly less than a peer "Head of" with HC. But the GTM and thought-leadership demands here go beyond a typical IC scope.

---

## Recommended counter

Counter at **180k base + 35k target bonus + commission on origination** (or partner-track milestone) and frame it explicitly:

> *« Le scope que vous décrivez — direction technique de mandats, conseil DSI, exécution commerciale, et build de la coreteam — correspond à un poste de Director chez vos comparables. Le package proposé positionne le rôle au niveau Tech Lead. Je propose qu'on aligne la rémunération avec le scope effectif. »*

If they're firm at 150k base, push hard on at least one of:

- (a) commission on deals I originate
- (b) a 12-month base review tied to commercial milestones
- (c) signing bonus + equity-equivalent if Talan group has any LTI vehicle
