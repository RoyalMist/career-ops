Your ‘day to day’

As Sr. Manager R&D Architecture, you will shape the end‑to‑end technology vision powering PMI’s R&D organization.

You’ll lead the definition of scalable, secure, and compliant architectures across Medical Science, Innovation, Quality, Regulatory, and Product Development—ensuring technology choices directly enable business impact and regulatory success.

Operating at the intersection of business strategy, digital innovation, and technology delivery, you’ll define the target‑state architecture for AI‑enabled R&D, accelerate high‑value use cases, and drive seamless integration across platforms such as LIMS, QMS, and OT/IoT. You’ll anticipate industry trends, guide modernization and application rationalization, and uphold architectural excellence through strong governance.

In this role, you’ll also build and inspire a high‑performing architecture team, collaborate closely with business and technology leaders, and partner with external experts—ensuring every architectural decision is evidence‑based, value‑driven, and aligned with PMI’s long‑term vision.

More specifically, you'll

    Define and steer Tech R&D architecture strategy, reference models, and multi-year roadmaps for the domain in scope.
    Assess technology trends and translate them into standards, patterns, and guardrails that accelerate value delivery while handling risk.
    Drive application rationalization to help the platforms on the technical debt management.
    Engage R&D leaders to map capabilities and prioritize architectural epics and investment.
    Shape decisions by presenting option analyses and NFR implications (reliability, scalability, performance, cybersecurity/OT safety), ensuring designs take in consideration operational constraints.
    Align global and local by governing solution choices across regions
    Establish and implement R&D architecture principles, ensuring their adherence across platforms by running architecture boards to approve domain and solution designs; monitor conformance and handle exceptions with clear risk visibility.
    Supervise risk and change in the R&D context; ensure compliance with internal standards and external regulations.
    Lead, develop, and coach a team of Domain Architects and Solution Architects; set priorities, supervise capacity, and foster a culture of business orientation, critical thinking, continuous learning, innovation, and autonomy.
    Build and foster an operating model where the Domain and Solution Architects:

- Act as the planning, integration, and communication focal point for new solutions within their domain.

- Provide architecture services insights; work on high level, cross functional initiatives ensuring alignment with business and IT strategies.

- Guide solutions within scope of the domain, providing oversight of more detailed solution architectures that are project/implementation driven.

- Provide oversight and leadership to Solution Architects in the domain.

- Curate input from the architecture community to establish principles, standards, and patterns used across IT products, aligned to enterprise direction.

  Build and foster an operating model where the Solution Architects:

- Own solution design and development across systems/platforms/applications to fulfill business needs.

- Serve as technical specialists on design, development, and implementation requirements.

- Ensure compatibility, interoperability, stability, usability, and performance of solutions.

  Advise and influence senior stakeholders; build strategic relationships internally and with key external partners to evolve the OPS architecture and accelerate delivery.
  Communicate progress, risks, and trade offs with clarity; ensure programs deliver on time, within scope, and on budget, in partnership with PMO and product/engineering leads.
