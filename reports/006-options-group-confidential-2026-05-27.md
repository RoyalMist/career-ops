    Build the strategic vision for the department

    Drive improvements and innovation

Requirements:

    10+ years Commodities experience in a trading environment

    Solid understanding of derivatives trading, including linear financial instruments and options
    Demonstrable understanding of AI and its potential use & benefit
    Stakeholder Management across multiple departments to C-Level
    Understanding technology best practice in terms of engineering and architectural processes
    Strong interpersonal skills
