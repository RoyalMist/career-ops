## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** senior specialist architect / operational systems engineer.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Head of Engineering / Director / Field CTO / strategic technology leader.

This is a local institutional architecture role, but the substance is close to operations engineering: SCADA administration, CDC system availability, troubleshooting, backups, maintenance, and on-call intervention. That makes it a poor use of application effort compared with current Geneva executive or public-sector Head-of-Service opportunities.

**If SIG is strategically interesting:** monitor for Head of IT, Chief Architect, digital transformation, cyber/resilience, enterprise architecture, data/AI, or IT service leadership roles. Do not use this posting as the entry point.
