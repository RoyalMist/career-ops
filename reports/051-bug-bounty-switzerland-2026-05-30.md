## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Mid-Senior Applied AI Engineer. The role reports to the CTO and owns AI capabilities, but it is still an individual contributor/build role rather than a leadership mandate.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering / AI Transformation Lead. The domain is attractive, but level and location are misaligned.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have built and led production platforms, and recently drove enterprise AI adoption and agentic workflows in Swiss companies."
- "My strongest contribution would be architecture and operating model for production AI systems, not only individual feature delivery."
- "I can go hands-on, but I would want to understand whether this role can evolve toward AI platform leadership."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept unless compensation/ESOP and growth path are unusually strong. Ask whether the company needs a Staff/Principal AI Engineer, AI Platform Lead or Head of Applied AI rather than a pure IC.
