## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Fractional IT support manager / operational consultant. LinkedIn labels it "Entry level", and the JD confirms a support-heavy scope: help desk, onboarding/offboarding, access rights, shared drives, backup, data hygiene, and troubleshooting.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Field CTO / VP Engineering / Head of Technology / Director Software Engineering. This is several levels below the target path.

**Sell senior without lying, if responding anyway**

- "I can help if the need is closer to fractional CIO/CTO: IT governance, security posture, vendor model, data/workflow automation, and a pragmatic roadmap."
- "For pure help desk and systems administration, I am likely over-scoped and would not be the most cost-effective profile."
- "If the organization wants to professionalize IT operations across a distributed team, I can define the operating model and hand it to a lighter support provider."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a standard support/admin scope. Only consider a short advisory package if the mandate is framed around IT governance, security, tool rationalization, automation, vendor selection, and a clear deliverable.
