**Level detected:** Senior Enterprise Architect / architecture authority. The LinkedIn "Mid-Senior" label understates the group-level architecture scope, but the role still appears IC rather than Director/Head.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Head of Engineering / Field CTO / Enterprise Architect. This is a legitimate adjacent target if Richemont gives the role real authority across domains, design governance and transformation roadmaps.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "My background is not classic ivory-tower enterprise architecture; it is architecture tied to delivery, governance, executive steering and regulated operations."
- "At FlowBank, I owned technology roadmap, budget, target architecture and execution indicators for the Executive Committee."
- "At Ripple / Metaco, I drove architecture across a regulated institutional platform while managing delivery, support, monitoring, QA and technical debt."
- "My added value for Richemont is combining enterprise architecture discipline with pragmatic delivery, AI adoption and governance."

**If they downlevel me**

Proceed only if the mandate includes design authority ownership, cross-domain influence, executive visibility, and compensation at least near the CHF 160k minimum. Decline if it becomes documentation-only architecture or internal standards policing without decision rights.
