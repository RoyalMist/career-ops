## C) Level and Strategy

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Field CTO / Head of Engineering / Director. This is one level too low unless Supabase treats the role as a strategic product owner with leadership visibility.

- "I have operated as a CTO/CPO and Head of Engineering, so I bring product judgement and infrastructure execution together."
- "I have owned roadmap, architecture, delivery, reliability and executive stakeholder alignment in regulated platforms."
- "My edge is translating platform constraints into business decisions: cost, capacity, reliability, enterprise readiness and adoption."

**If they downlevel me**

Proceed only if compensation and equity are strong, the role has founder/product leadership exposure, and there is a path toward Core Product Lead, Head of Platform Product, Field CTO, or Product/Engineering leadership.
