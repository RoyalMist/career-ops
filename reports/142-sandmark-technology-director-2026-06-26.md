**Level detected:** Technology Director for a young Geneva crypto media/data company, likely Head-of-Tech/CTO minus formal executive title.

**Candidate's natural level:** CTO / VP Engineering / Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering.

**Sell senior without lying:**

- "I have already owned the full technology function for a FINMA-regulated digital bank, including roadmap, budget, providers, data platform and cloud transition."
- "I can be hands-on enough to understand architecture, trade-offs and delivery blockers, while structuring the team so the company does not depend on heroics."
- "Digital assets are not a buzzword in my profile; I led engineering for institutional blockchain custody at Ripple / Metaco."

**If they downlevel the role:** accept only if scope and package are coherent. This should not become a hands-on delivery manager role with a Director title. Require ownership of roadmap, architecture, budget, team design, build-vs-buy, vendor strategy and production quality.
