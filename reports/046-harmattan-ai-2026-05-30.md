## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Mid-Senior Engineering Manager. The JD asks for people management, recruitment, process standardization, internal tooling, and organizational support. It does not clearly give ownership of product strategy, architecture, budget, or a whole engineering function.

**Candidate natural level:** Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering / CTO. The match is strong on people and operating model, but weaker on level.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have already led engineering organizations up to 50 people and managed managers, tech leads, hiring, levelling, coaching, quality, release and support operating models."
- "My strongest contribution would be building the management layer and operating discipline that lets Harmattan scale without losing execution speed."
- "I do not come from aeronautics. I do come from regulated, security-sensitive, high-stakes platforms where reliability, governance and fast delivery all matter."

**If they downlevel**

Accept only if the role has cross-module authority, a clear route to Head/Director scope, meaningful equity/bonus upside, and direct leadership access. Do not accept a pure recruiting/performance-review role.
