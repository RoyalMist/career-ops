## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior strategic delivery lead. The role is a senior IC / programme owner with high trust, cross-functional authority and customer-facing accountability, not a people-management executive role.

**Candidate natural level:** Field CTO / AI transformation leader / Director-level technology executive. This is close if OpenAI treats the SDL as accountable for strategic public-sector outcomes, weaker if it becomes delivery PMO.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "My strongest fit is the bridge role: executive stakeholder translation, technical scoping, delivery governance and adoption for AI in regulated environments."
- "At Wonderful, I led AI platform demos and adoption discussions with C-level and technical audiences in Swiss regulated contexts."
- "At SCRYPT, I implemented Claude Enterprise through Slack, Linear, email and calendars, which gives me a concrete adoption story rather than abstract AI strategy."
- "At Ripple / Metaco and FlowBank, I owned regulated technology delivery where trust, security, governance and client confidence were part of the product."

**If they downlevel me**

Do not accept a pure programme-manager framing. Proceed only if the mandate includes ownership of customer outcomes, milestone acceptance, executive communication, delivery patterns, and visible influence on how OpenAI for Government scales internationally.
