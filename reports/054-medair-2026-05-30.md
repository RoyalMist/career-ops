## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Global Head of IT / senior IT manager. The role has meaningful scope: IT strategy, governance, cybersecurity, platform harmonisation, cloud-first strategy and management of ICT plus Digital Solutions/Engineering. The title is "Manager", but the mandate reads like a non-profit Head of IT.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for the scope, especially FlowBank + Ripple/Metaco + UBP. The main mismatch is not capability; it is likely package, culture, and whether the role has enough strategic authority.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have already led a full technology department of approximately 40 people in a regulated Swiss environment, with roadmap, budget, service delivery, cloud transition and executive reporting."
- "My strongest fit is connecting IT governance, cybersecurity, architecture and service delivery so technology supports the mission instead of becoming fragmented."
- "I do not come from humanitarian operations, but I have worked in environments where trust, continuity, security and stakeholder alignment are non-negotiable."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a pure operational IT manager role if the accountability includes global architecture, cyber risk, country programme support and executive reporting. Only continue if the package, decision rights and team/budget scope reflect the global mandate.
