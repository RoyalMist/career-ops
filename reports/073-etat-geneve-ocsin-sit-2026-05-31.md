## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Head of Service / public-sector IT infrastructure leader. The mandate is not just technical architecture; it owns service delivery, sectors, budgets, recruitment/external resources, public procurement, controls and compliance.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong. This maps to FlowBank Global Head of Technology plus Pictet platform/SRE work and Ripple/Metaco operational excellence.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "J'ai dirige une direction technologique d'environ 40 personnes couvrant engineering, data, infrastructure, service delivery et fournisseurs externes dans une banque FINMA."
- "Mon angle fort pour ce service est l'industrialisation: cloud, IaC, CI/CD, DevSecOps, supervision, reduction de l'obsolescence et gouvernance des risques."
- "Je n'ai pas pilote directement des marches publics genevois; en revanche, j'ai gere budgets, renouvellements fournisseurs, prestataires externes et arbitrages capacitaires dans des environnements regules."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a pure infrastructure manager interpretation if the accountability remains class-25 Head of Service. The role should include decision rights over budget, sector organization, public procurement, service catalog, platform modernization, risk controls and direct DSI exposure.
