## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Global Director, commercial technology adoption and enablement.

**Candidate's natural level:** CTO / Field CTO / VP Engineering / Director Software Engineering / AI transformation lead. This role is aligned on executive communication, customer adoption and technology-to-business-value, but it is less aligned with hands-on ownership of product/engineering strategy.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have repeatedly translated complex technology into business value for executive and technical audiences, from enterprise AI platforms to regulated fintech infrastructure."
- "My strongest contribution is operating at the bridge between product, engineering, customers and leadership, turning capabilities into adoption plans and measurable outcomes."
- "I do not come from the construction-equipment dealer ecosystem, but I have built operating models in regulated, high-trust environments where domain understanding and adoption discipline mattered."
- "I have led teams up to 50 people and worked with product, field engineering and institutional clients to turn technical capabilities into delivery outcomes."

**If they downlevel the scope**

Do not accept a role that is mostly sales training, dealer content creation or field enablement execution. It must include global decision rights, a senior team, commercial strategy influence, product/engineering partnership and compensation consistent with director scope.
