## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior pre-sales Solutions Architect / Applied AI Architect. It reports through sales/applied AI rather than engineering management, but it has high leverage with enterprise customers and product feedback.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for Field CTO / Enterprise AI Solutions Leader. Less strong if Anthropic evaluates this as a German-speaking DACH sales engineering role first and an AI architecture role second.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "My closest fit is Field CTO: I have led enterprise AI demonstrations, translated platform capabilities into business value, and connected executive adoption to technical architecture."
- "I have implemented Claude Enterprise inside an organization with Slack, Linear, email and calendar workflows, so I can speak from deployment experience rather than slideware."
- "I bring regulated Swiss context from banking, custody, audit and cloud platforms, which matters for Claude adoption in financial services."
- "German is the point to clarify. I am French native and fully professional in English; my German is limited working, so I would need to understand the customer territory."

**If they downlevel**

Accept only if compensation and scope reflect Anthropic's frontier-AI premium: enterprise customers, architecture authority, evals/deployment ownership, product feedback loop and credible growth path to Applied AI lead/manager/Field CTO-equivalent scope. Do not accept a generic sales engineer framing.
