## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior Enterprise Architect / principal individual contributor. The JD is strategic and senior, but not executive: it reports to the Head of Architecture and Solution Design and focuses on architecture guidance rather than owning an IT function.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering, with enterprise architecture as a secondary fit. The candidate can do the role, but it likely underuses management, budget and operating-model experience.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have built target architectures and roadmaps in regulated Swiss environments, including a FINMA-regulated neobank and institutional custody platform."
- "My value is architecture that is executable: roadmap, governance, budget awareness, delivery model, operational risk and stakeholder alignment."
- "I do not have aviation domain tenure. I would ramp up through business capabilities such as operational safety, security and industry services, while bringing regulated systems discipline."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a pure architecture IC role unless compensation is strong, the mandate has real enterprise influence, and there is a clear path toward Head of Architecture / Director of Technology scope.
