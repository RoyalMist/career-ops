Purpose of the role: 

Proton continues to grow rapidly as increasing numbers of people across the globe seek greater privacy controls of their information online. To accelerate our growth, we are seeking a senior leader to drive engineering for one of our many existing or upcoming Products.

We are seeking a Director of Engineering to one of our existing or upcoming Products. This role involves setting and executing technical strategy, team management, and working closely with your senior product managers counterparts, to ensure high-velocity delivery of new products.

As a key member of the engineering leadership team, you will shape the company’s technical strategy and contribute to Proton’s mission of creating a privacy-centric internet. Each product will have its unique challenges and areas of focus, nevertheless we require all Engineering Directors for our Products to have a demonstrated track record of building and scaling customer facing applications, in either a B2C or B2B context, for millions of users, across multiple platforms (mobile, desktop, backend systems and platforms).

In addition to technical expertise, Engineering Directors as senior leaders within Proton, have excellent communication skills, a business mindset, and a strong sense of empathy for the end customers.

What you will do:

    Setting technical direction and vision for one of our Products
    Lead technical design and platform discussions
    Drive continuous improvement in our DevSecOps practices, and improve lead times and time to market while maintaining exceptional standards of product quality
    Hold yourself and team accountable for timely delivery of scalable, quality-focus user experiences and features
    Develop efficient processes, and motivate your team to operate with high velocity, ownership, and accountability
    Lead through others, and develop the next generation of engineering leaders at Proton
    Inspire, coach, and develop your teams
    Optimize your organizational structure, and continuously raise the bar on hiring
    Build strong relationships with senior leaders, and advance your long-term platform and engineering vision
    Contribute to Proton's technology and open source communities
    Foster an inclusive and diverse environment for your team to show up and give their best each day

    Applied cryptography (PGP, TLS, etc.)
    REST APIs
    Event architectures
    HTTP networking
    SQL, Redis
    PHP (Symfony), Rust, Python
    iOS, macOS (Swift)
    Android (Kotlin, Jetpack)
    Web (Typescript, React)
    Desktop (C#, Swift)
    Infrastructure: MySQL / Ceph

    Experience in software engineering in progressively complex roles
    Experience directly leading and managing engineers
    Demonstrable experience as a manager of managers
    A history of collaboration with senior leaders in engineering, product on developing technical and product strategy
    Great empathy and collaboration skills, and even greater resilience
    A curious, passionate, and growth-oriented mindset
    Deep experience as a hands-on software engineer creating well-crafted, maintainable systems
    Mobile-first background (ideally former mobile developer)
    Practical experience leading DevOps and SRE initiatives and driving best practices
    Experience with cryptography preferred
    Bonus points for each of the following:

        Experience with performance tuning and scaling across client, backend, infrastructure
