## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Executive committee role, likely equivalent to a business/digital transformation director rather than a CIO/CTO. The Development domain includes IT, PMO, network development and innovation, with roughly 100 employees.

**Candidate's natural level:** CTO, VP Engineering, Head/Director Engineering, Field CTO, AI transformation leader.

This is a strong level match but an adjacent function match. It is more attractive than the prior tpg Key-user / Product Owner evaluation because this mandate is executive, strategic and innovation-led. It buys digital mobility, AI adoption, IT orientation, transformation, partnerships, budget and performance management. It is not a software-engineering leadership role.

**Positioning:**

- "I bring executive technology leadership to organizations where digital capability must become operational transformation."
- "My strongest contribution is turning AI, data, architecture and operating model choices into measurable institutional outcomes."
- "At FlowBank, I owned roadmap, budget and executive indicators for a regulated digital institution; at SCRYPT and Wonderful, I translated AI into practical adoption and workflow change."
- "For tpg, I would not come as a mobility expert from day one; I would come as a digital transformation executive who can make innovation, IT and public-service constraints work together."

**If they downlevel the mandate:** do not pursue if the role becomes mainly PMO reporting, innovation theatre, or political stakeholder coordination without real authority over IT/digital mobility, budget, portfolio and domain leadership.
