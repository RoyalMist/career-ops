## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Technical Team Lead / squad-level leadership.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO / VP Engineering / Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering / strategic Enterprise Architect.

This is likely a downlevel. The local Meyrin location, French/English requirement and React/Java/product-platform content are positives, but the mandate is not broad enough: 7-person team, backlog, timesheets, budget consumption, release plans, support coordination and reporting. It sounds like a strong hands-on delivery lead role inside a product area, not a senior technology leadership mandate.

**Sell senior without lying if applying anyway:**

- "I have led much larger regulated engineering organizations, but I can be useful where a platform team needs stronger delivery, release quality, architecture alignment and stakeholder communication."
- "My experience is broader than content platforms, but I have direct web/platform engineering roots and have led teams through quality, release, support and roadmap discipline."
- "I would want to understand whether this role has room to grow toward Product Area / Head-level responsibility."

**If they downlevel you:** pass unless compensation and grade are surprisingly strong, or Richemont frames it as a path into broader Product Area leadership within 6-12 months with written criteria.
