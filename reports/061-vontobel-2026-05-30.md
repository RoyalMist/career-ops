## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior hands-on AI architecture leader. This is not a CTO/VP role, but it has enterprise-wide AI architecture ownership and regulated AI platform scope.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for Field CTO / Enterprise AI Architect / AI Transformation Lead. Slightly less natural if they expect deep daily Python/MLOps implementation.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led AI adoption and enterprise AI architecture discussions in Swiss regulated environments, with concrete work around RAG, agents, workflow integration and governance."
- "My value is strongest at the junction of architecture, regulated delivery, stakeholder alignment and practical production constraints."
- "I am not a pure ML research profile; I am an architecture and delivery leader for production-grade AI systems."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a narrow senior IC architect role unless the scope is genuinely enterprise-wide and compensation reflects strategic AI ownership. Ask for decision rights over AI standards, platform patterns, model-risk controls, architecture reviews and guild leadership.
