## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Founder-level CTO, very hands-on.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for CTO/product/architecture/security/client-facing leadership. Weak fit for a hands-on founder CTO role when the implementation stack is already selected and the immediate need is individual-contributor execution.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have already operated as CTO/CPO and built a Swiss production product end-to-end, including backend, PostgreSQL, APIs, mobile delivery, cloud and AI-enabled product work."
- "My strongest fit is the combination you need: financial systems, customer-facing product decisions, security/auditability, AI workflows, and practical architecture."
- "I would want to validate stack and decision rights. I am not looking for a founder CTO role where the stack is already fixed and the main mandate is to implement inside it."

**If they downlevel me**

Do not accept a "co-founder" label without co-founder economics and CTO-level decision rights. If salary is low or zero, equity must be meaningful, with vesting, governance, founder protections, and decision rights. If equity is small, or if the stack/architecture is already set and the work is mostly coding, treat it as founding engineer, not CTO/co-founder.
