## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Oracle M3 Senior Manager. This can be substantial, but the JD is too generic to distinguish between meaningful multi-team leadership and routine middle management.

**Candidate natural level:** Director / Head of Engineering / CTO. Candidate is qualified for engineering management, but the role may be a downlevel unless it includes managers, multiple teams, platform/product ownership, budget and strategic roadmap.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led engineering and technology organizations up to 50 people, including managers and tech leads, in regulated fintech and crypto environments."
- "My strongest contribution is not only delivery management; it is architecture, operating model, team design, quality, reliability and executive alignment."
- "Before investing further, I would like to understand whether this M3 role has Director-track scope: number of teams, manager layer, product area, decision rights and roadmap ownership."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a narrow single-team manager role unless compensation is exceptional and the product area is unusually strategic. Prefer roles with Head/Director title or explicit multi-team authority.
