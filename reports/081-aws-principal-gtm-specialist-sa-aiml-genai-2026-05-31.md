## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Principal Specialist Solutions Architect, likely L6/L7-style senior IC depending AWS leveling.

**Candidate natural level:** strong Field CTO / Principal AI Solutions Architect / Director-level technical leader.

This is exactly the kind of senior IC role that should *not* be discarded by default: it has executive customer exposure, cross-org influence, product roadmap feedback, EMEA scale, thought leadership, strategic account escalation and AI/GenAI domain focus. It is closer to Field CTO than implementation SA.

**Location strategy**

The application should not say "I can relocate to Zurich." The right position is:

> I am Geneva-based and already engaged in Swiss enterprise AI/cloud discussions from Romandie. I saw the role listed in Zurich, but AWS Switzerland also has Geneva presence and other AWS roles are currently posted in Geneva. Would this Principal EMEA specialist role support a Geneva base with travel to Zurich/customers/EMEA as needed?

If the answer is no and the cadence is Zurich-heavy, do not proceed unless the package and mandate are exceptional.
