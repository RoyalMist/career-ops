## C) Level and Strategy

Detected level: Senior IC / lead transformation role, not Head/Director/CTO.

Candidate natural level: CTO, Field CTO, Head/Director Engineering, AI transformation leader.

This role is not executive enough for the original north-star track, but it is one of the strongest examples of the emerging second path: fewer people-management obligations, more direct operational impact, local regulated fintech context, and AI/automation work close to real business workflows.

The key question is whether it is a sane lead role or a hidden pressure cooker. "Compliance first", "production environments", "fast-growing", "high-autonomy" and only 40% remote could still create heavy cognitive load.
