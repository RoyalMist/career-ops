## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior AI Solutions Architect / agentic systems architect. The role owns architecture and production delivery for a domain-specific finance AI platform, but it is not a formal people-management role.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for Agentic / Automation, AI Solutions Architect and fintech/blockchain technology leadership. The role is below formal CTO/VP/Head level, but it has enough domain leverage to be worth screening.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led regulated fintech and crypto technology organizations, but I am also comfortable building enough of the system myself to make architecture real."
- "My differentiator is the combination: blockchain custody, banking/audit controls, cloud platforms and practical Claude-based workflow automation."
- "I have not owned month-end close operations as a finance manager; what I bring is the architecture and operating model to automate high-trust workflows safely with finance SMEs."
- "For agentic systems, I care less about demos and more about guardrails: data classification, human checkpoints, audit logs, failure detection and rollback."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a generic automation developer scope. Continue only if the level includes platform ownership, finance-domain architecture, CoE influence, senior stakeholder reporting, production guardrails and compensation near the user's executive target.
