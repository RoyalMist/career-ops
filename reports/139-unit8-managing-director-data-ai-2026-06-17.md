## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Managing Director / Partner-track leadership in a specialist Data & AI consultancy.

**Candidate natural level:** Executive technology leader / Field CTO / Head of Engineering / AI transformation lead. The seniority is right. The commercial profile is adjacent rather than exact.

**Sell senior without lying**

- Lead with Field CTO, Head of Engineering, and Director Software Engineering experience rather than hands-on engineering.
- Use this positioning: "I bridge C-suite AI ambition, technical delivery, and operating model change in regulated Swiss environments."
- Anchor on Wonderful for enterprise AI presales, SCRYPT for AI adoption embedded into Slack/Linear/email/calendar workflows, FlowBank for executive roadmap/budget ownership, and Ripple / Metaco for institutional client delivery.
- Be explicit that the strongest network is Swiss/Romandie and regulated finance, not German-speaking pharma or chemicals.

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a pure delivery lead or consulting manager variant. This only makes sense if the mandate includes real leadership-team influence, client ownership, and compensation near executive target. A six-month scope review is not enough if the German/network requirements are fundamental.
