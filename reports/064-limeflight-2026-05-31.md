## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** True Head of Engineering / Director-level operating role. Not CTO, but senior enough if decision rights are real.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong. This maps well to Director Software Engineering, Head of Engineering and CTO/CPO experience, especially scaling from founder-led engineering to a more structured organization.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have already done the transition from strong engineers to a more scalable operating model: squads, leads, support/L3 ownership, quality practices, roadmap planning and leadership coaching."
- "My technical stack is broader than Microsoft-specific. I can challenge architecture and reliability decisions, but I would want strong .NET/Azure leads in the team if the role requires deep stack-specific code review."
- "My edge is operating discipline without bureaucracy: clarity, ownership, delivery predictability, quality, reliability and useful leadership layers."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept if the role becomes a hands-on engineering manager under founder control. Accept only if compensation and decision rights match the Head mandate: leadership layers, hiring, performance, delivery predictability, reliability, architecture/security standards and headcount planning.
