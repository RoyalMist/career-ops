## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Director of internal business technology and AI enablement. It is senior, but more business systems / internal platform leadership than product engineering.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong for AI Transformation Lead, Head of IT / Business Technology, and enterprise operating-model leadership. Less ideal only if they expect a pure hands-on IT operations manager.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led technology organizations in regulated and distributed environments, including roadmap, budget, vendor governance, service delivery, architecture and executive stakeholder alignment."
- "At SCRYPT, I already implemented the kind of AI enablement this role describes: Claude Enterprise integrated with Slack, Linear, email and calendars, connected to executive and cross-functional workflows."
- "My strength is turning tools into operating systems: ownership, access model, workflow design, cost discipline, security posture and measurable adoption."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a role that is mostly helpdesk/device management with AI as a side project. Accept only if the mandate includes SaaS governance, AI enablement, budget/spend, business systems, security partnership and senior stakeholder decision-making.
