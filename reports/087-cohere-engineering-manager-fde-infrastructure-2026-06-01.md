## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Engineering Manager / deployment leader.

**Candidate natural level:** Director/Head/Field CTO.

This clears the bar because it is not a generic squad EM. The role sits in enterprise AI, owns customer deployment outcomes, manages FDEs, and likely has executive/customer exposure. The caveat is the JD's own warning: this is "not a typical management role" and expects the manager to lead from the front. That can be acceptable if it means technical credibility and escalation ownership; it is less attractive if it means day-to-day ticket execution.

**Qualify early:**
- Can Cohere employ from Switzerland, not only EU/UK?
- Team size and reporting line.
- Expected hands-on ratio.
- Compensation band and equity.
- Travel / customer-site expectations.
