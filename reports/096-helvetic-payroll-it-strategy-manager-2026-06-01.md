## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Manager / senior consultant / transformation office role.

**Candidate natural level:** Head of Technology, Director Software Engineering, Field CTO, CTO/CPO.

This is a strong capability match but a weaker level match. The JD asks for 5-10 years and emphasizes PowerPoint, Excel, strategy-to-execution, KPI tracking and governance support. The candidate can do this, but it may underuse executive engineering leadership and turn into internal consulting/PMO work.

**Sell senior without lying plan:**

- Lead with FlowBank: technology roadmap, budget, architecture and Executive Committee indicators.
- Position SCRYPT/Wonderful as practical AI adoption and executive-facing transformation.
- Use Ripple / Metaco for operating model, platform resilience, L3/service management and cross-functional delivery.
- Keep the tone pragmatic: "I can help turn strategy into executable technology operating model."

**If they downlevel me plan:**

Only proceed if:

- Day rate / total compensation is strong enough to justify a contract role.
- The client is known and attractive.
- The reporting line is senior technology or operations leadership.
- The work includes real transformation decisions, not only slide production and progress tracking.
