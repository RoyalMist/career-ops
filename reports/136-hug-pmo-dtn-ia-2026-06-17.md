## C) Level and Strategy

Detected level: senior institutional portfolio and governance role. It is not a generic project-manager job: the posting says the PMO is a member of the DTN-IA management committee and deputy to the DTN-IA direction, with responsibility for the global portfolio of roughly 150 digital projects per year and the IT investment budget.

Candidate natural level: CTO, Field CTO, Head/Director Engineering, AI transformation leader. This role is adjacent rather than direct. It passes the profile's PMO exception only because it appears to include enterprise operating-model authority, governance of a major digital portfolio, and proximity to institutional digital/AI strategy.

Positioning:

- Lead with "technology executive who can govern a large digital transformation portfolio."
- Emphasize the ability to align strategy, budget, risk, resource capacity and delivery reality.
- Use regulated-finance experience as the governance and risk analogue for hospital systems.
- Keep AI as a relevant differentiator, but do not pretend the role is an AI delivery lead.
- Frame the move as institutional impact in Geneva healthcare, not a step down into reporting.
