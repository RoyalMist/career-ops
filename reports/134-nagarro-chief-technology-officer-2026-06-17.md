Nagarro is looking for the "Thinker" in a leadership triad: someone who shapes market narratives, advises global financial institutions, authors PoVs, speaks externally, and influences strategic deals.

Candidate natural level: Field CTO, CTO, Head/Director Engineering, AI transformation leader. The conceptual role is attractive because it buys exactly the candidate's mix of executive technology, regulated finance, client advisory, architecture and AI adoption.

However, two posted constraints dominate the decision:

- Fluent German is required for DACH executive engagement.
- The role requires being based in Germany or Austria near a Nagarro office.

Given the user's German is limited working and location is Geneva, the role should not be prioritized unless the recruiter explicitly reframes the mandate.

If challenged, the honest positioning would be:

- "I am strong for BFSI technology transformation, AI adoption, regulated platforms and executive advisory."
- "My strongest territory is Switzerland/Romandie/international English-speaking work, not German-speaking DACH coverage."
- "I can contribute to BFSI PoVs and advisory, but I am not a public keynote/published thought leader in the way the JD describes."
