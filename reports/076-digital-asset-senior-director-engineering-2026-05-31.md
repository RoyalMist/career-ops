## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior Director / near-VP Engineering. This is a leader-of-leaders role focused on engineering operating model rather than pure architecture.

**Candidate natural level:** Very strong. The role maps directly to Ripple / Metaco Director Software Engineering, FlowBank Global Head of Technology and SCRYPT Head of Engineering. The regulated blockchain domain is unusually aligned.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "At Ripple / Metaco, I led up to 50 professionals across Geneva and San Francisco on a regulated blockchain custody platform for institutional financial clients."
- "My strongest contribution is turning high-caliber engineering teams into a reliable delivery system: planning, release discipline, quality, L3 support, technical debt, career frameworks and executive visibility."
- "I am technical enough to partner with architects and engineers, but I do not need to be the CTO owning the deepest protocol decisions. I am strongest where delivery, people, quality and operating cadence meet."
- "I have not built directly on Canton/Daml, but I have led regulated blockchain custody engineering and understand the institutional trust, security and delivery constraints."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept below Senior Director / VP-equivalent scope. The mandate must include leader-of-leaders authority, planning/release operating model, budget influence, career frameworks and meaningful CTO/CPO partnership. If they want a local Zurich delivery manager with limited authority, pass.
