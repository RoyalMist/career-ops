## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Head of IT, member of the management committee, reporting to the Director. The formal scope is senior, but the 5-person team suggests Swiss mid-market Head of IT rather than large-enterprise CIO.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong fit for Head of IT / CTO in Romandie. The candidate has operated at larger team scope, but this mandate has attractive decision proximity, real operational responsibility, and local energy/infrastructure impact.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "J'ai deja pilote une fonction technologique complete dans une banque FINMA: roadmap, budget, architecture cible, infrastructure, service delivery, fournisseurs et indicateurs pour le Comite Executif."
- "Mon apport est de structurer une IT pragmatique: gouvernance, cybersecurite, architecture logicielle, qualite de service, fournisseurs, budget et priorisation avec les metiers."
- "Je n'ai pas une carriere utilities pure; mes experiences les plus proches sont les environnements reglementes, les systemes operationnels, la commodity/trade finance, l'audit IT/securite et l'infrastructure cloud."

**If they downlevel me**

Do not accept if this becomes a hands-on IT manager role paid like a local support manager. Consider it if the package is near or above CHF 180k, reporting line is truly to the Director, budget and vendor decisions are real, and the role owns the transformation roadmap rather than only ticket/backlog execution.
