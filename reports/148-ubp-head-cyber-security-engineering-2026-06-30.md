## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Head of Cyber Security Engineering. This is a senior functional leadership role with roadmap, team, budget, vendors and platform lifecycle ownership.

**Candidate's natural level:** Head of Engineering / Director Software Engineering / CTO-adjacent regulated-platform leader (`config/profile.yml:15-42`). The level is credible, though narrower than a full CTO mandate.

**Sell senior without lying:**

- "I have led regulated technology organizations and platform teams where security, operational resilience and compliance were not optional."
- "My strongest value is building the engineering operating model around secure platforms: roadmap, budget, vendor strategy, automation, SLAs, KPIs and team ownership."
- "I bring a rare combination for UBP: prior UBP audit context, FINMA-regulated banking technology leadership, institutional custody engineering and hands-on cloud/DevSecOps background."

**If they downlevel me plan:** Avoid a role that becomes pure security-platform product owner or hands-on security engineer. Accept only if it truly owns cyber engineering roadmap, team leadership, vendor strategy and budget. Ask reporting line, team size and current platform maturity early.
