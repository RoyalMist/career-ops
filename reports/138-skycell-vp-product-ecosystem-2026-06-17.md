## C) Level and Strategy

Detected level: VP Product / senior product ecosystem executive. The role owns the product vision and portfolio strategy across physical containers, IoT infrastructure, digital platforms, connectivity, data intelligence and customer experience. It partners closely with the Co-Founder & CTO, who appears to retain stronger hardware-container focus.

Candidate natural level: CTO, CPTO, Head/Director Engineering, Field CTO, AI transformation leader. This role is adjacent and plausible because the candidate has CPTO experience, product-technology leadership, AI adoption, cloud/data platforms and regulated systems. It is not as clean a fit as Head of Engineering or Deputy CTO because it likely requires deeper product management, product-market fit, pricing, adoption and customer-segment ownership.

Positioning:

- Lead with CPTO/product-technology leadership, not pure engineering management.
- Emphasize ability to bridge CTO, engineering, commercial teams and customer value.
- Use Bazzile as the anchor for product ownership and end-to-end platform build.
- Use FlowBank/Pictet for data/cloud/platform maturity.
- Use Wonderful/SCRYPT for AI/data-enabled product and adoption insight.
- Be honest that pharma logistics and IoT hardware are adjacent, not direct prior domains.
