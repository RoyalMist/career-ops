## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Head of Engineering / Director metadata, but operational scope is a 7-9 person engineering group inside an agency's Experience & Engineering function.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO, VP Engineering, Director Software Engineering, Head of Engineering, Field CTO, or AI Transformation Lead. The title fits, but the scope is probably below the candidate's strongest lane.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led engineering organizations up to 50 people, including managers and tech leads, and I am strongest when the role combines operating model, architecture and executive/client alignment."
- "My AI angle is practical engineering transformation: Claude Enterprise, Slack, Linear, agentic workflows, RAG demos and AI-assisted delivery practices."
- "I can help DEPT move AI-driven development from tool adoption to repeatable engineering rituals, governance and measurable delivery improvement."
- "I bring both client-facing AI platform experience and internal engineering organization design."

**If they downscope me**

Only continue if the mandate has clear DACH-level engineering strategy ownership, not just local team management. Ask for:

1. Reporting line and decision rights.
2. Actual team growth plan over 12 months.
3. Expected utilization/billability responsibilities.
4. Language expectations for German client and internal work.
5. Compensation range before investing in interviews.
