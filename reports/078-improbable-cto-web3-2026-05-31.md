## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Foundational CTO for a venture-backed Web3/L1 platform. This is more founder-CTO / protocol CTO than enterprise Director Engineering.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong as a technology executive, partial as a Web3 CTO. The leadership, product and regulated blockchain pieces fit, but DeFi/DEX/on-chain product depth is the weak point.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I led engineering for a regulated blockchain custody platform for institutional clients at Ripple / Metaco, including architecture, quality, support and client delivery."
- "My strongest Web3 angle is institutional trust, custody, security and delivery discipline; I do not want to overstate DeFi/DEX protocol-native experience."
- "I can lead the engineering organization and product/architecture trade-offs, but would rely on strong protocol and DeFi specialists for the deepest domain decisions."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a "CTO" label that is actually developer relations or hands-on ecosystem engineering without authority. Proceed only if the mandate includes technical strategy, team leadership, roadmap, architecture, external representation, and meaningful equity/base economics.
