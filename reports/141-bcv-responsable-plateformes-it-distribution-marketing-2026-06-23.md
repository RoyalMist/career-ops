## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Chef de service / service head for IT platforms in BCV's IT departments. The team size and budget/portfolio wording are materially stronger than an IC architect or product-owner role.

**Candidate's natural level:** CTO, VP Engineering, Head/Director Engineering, Field CTO, strategic Enterprise Architect.

This role is slightly below the target lane but not a bad downlevel. It buys real management, delivery accountability, budget/reporting, platform evolution and banking stakeholder work. It is probably closer to a department/service leadership role than the previous BCV Product Manager or Architecte logiciel expert evaluations.

**Sell senior without lying**

- Lead with FlowBank: FINMA-regulated neobank, ~40-person technology department, ExCo roadmap/budget, CRM/Core Banking, AWS, real-time data platform.
- Use Ripple / Metaco for leadership scale: 50 professionals, managers/tech leads, regulated institutional custody, architecture and support operating model.
- Use UBP for banking governance, risk, GDPR and French/English reporting.
- Mention SCRYPT/Wonderful AI only as a differentiator for future platform automation, not as the center of the application.
- Translate executive leadership into "chef de service capable de cadrer, arbitrer et fiabiliser la delivery avec les metiers Front".

**If they downlevel or the package is weak**

Accept a process only if BCV confirms meaningful decision rights over platform strategy, budget, delivery practices and team leadership. If the role is mainly Jira/project follow-up around Dynamics and campaign tools, it should be discarded despite the title.
