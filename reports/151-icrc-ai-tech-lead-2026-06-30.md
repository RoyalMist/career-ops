## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior technical lead / AI systems architect under a Data & AI Technology Manager.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO, Field CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, Director Software Engineering, or strategic AI transformation/enterprise architecture role ([config/profile.yml](/Users/prism/Work/career-ops/config/profile.yml:15)).

**Strategy:** Treat this as a qualified maybe, not a top-priority application. The selling angle is not "I am the best PyTorch model trainer"; it is "I can make AI usable, secure, deployable, and governed in a complex humanitarian organization."

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led production platforms, cloud/Kubernetes infrastructure, AI adoption, and regulated technology organizations; my value is connecting AI architecture with delivery, governance, and operational resilience."
- "I can work with data scientists and ML specialists while owning the production architecture, integration model, security posture, and delivery discipline."
- "My background is strongest in applied AI systems, RAG/agentic workflows, platform engineering, and regulated delivery rather than academic ML research."

**If they downlevel**

Accept only if:
- Compensation is at least CHF 150k and preferably CHF 160k+.
- The role has visible architecture authority across ICRC AI platforms.
- The hands-on expectation is architecture/prototyping/integration, not mostly model training.
- There is a path to broader Data & AI leadership.
