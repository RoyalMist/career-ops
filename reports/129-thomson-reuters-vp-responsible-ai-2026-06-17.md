## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** VP, firmwide AI governance and transformation, reporting to Global Chief Data & Analytics Officer.

**Candidate's natural level:** CTO / Field CTO / VP Engineering / AI Transformation Lead. This is senior enough and strategically aligned, but it is less pure product/engineering leadership and more governance, risk, adoption and enterprise control design.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "My background is not only AI experimentation; it is AI adoption in high-trust environments where governance, security, auditability and operational ownership matter."
- "I have led organizations up to 50 people and built operating models across engineering, product, field/client teams, risk and executive leadership."
- "At SCRYPT I embedded Claude Enterprise into executive and cross-functional workflows; at UBP I learned how governance and evidence work in regulated banking; at Ripple/Metaco I led secure institutional platform delivery."
- "Responsible AI at scale needs the same ingredients as regulated platform delivery: inventory, ownership, controls, observability, incident handling, audit evidence and adoption discipline."

**If they downlevel the scope**

Do not accept if the role is mostly policy writing without authority over tools, SDLC/MLOps integration, team design and executive accountability. It must include the 30-person team, platform/tooling influence, and business-unit adoption power.
