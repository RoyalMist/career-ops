## C) Level and Strategy

Detected level: executive operational CTO #2. The role owns execution while the CTO focuses on long-range AI transformation and external representation. It includes authority over delivery, resilience, organizational health, budget, platform consolidation, shared data capability, regulatory execution and VP mentoring.

Candidate natural level: CTO, Deputy CTO, Head of Engineering, Director Software Engineering, regulated fintech/crypto technology leader. This is a strong level match.

The role is especially aligned with:

- Ripple / Metaco regulated crypto custody leadership;
- FlowBank bank technology roadmap, budget and real-time data platform;
- SCRYPT AI-enabled engineering operating model;
- UBP audit, GDPR and control thinking;
- executive communication across clients, leadership and regulated stakeholders.

The fit is not perfect because the posting leans into market-making and low-latency trading infrastructure. The candidate has strong financial technology and crypto custody proof, but not obvious HFT/Rust/trading-engine ownership.

Positioning:

- Lead with "operational CTO for regulated fintech and digital assets."
- Emphasize execution cadence, platform modernization, security/compliance-by-design and data governance.
- Use Ripple / Metaco and FlowBank as the core spine of the story.
- Present low-latency trading as adjacent but not overstated.
- Treat company identity and remote eligibility as hard gates.
