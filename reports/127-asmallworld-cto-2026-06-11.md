## C) Level and Strategy

Detected level: Executive CTO with direct CEO reporting line.

Candidate natural level: CTO / CPTO / Head of Engineering / Field CTO.

This is aligned with the primary target lane. The best positioning is "commercial CTO who can modernize a lean stack, connect CRM/data/product engineering to growth, and add practical AI automation without losing governance." Do not over-index on fintech. Lead with Bazzile, FlowBank, SCRYPT and Ripple / Metaco.

The main risk is hidden downleveling: the JD explicitly asks for someone who will roll up sleeves, write/review code and quickly test innovations. That can be acceptable for a small company CTO, but only if the role has real product/technology authority, budget control, hiring authority and executive compensation.
