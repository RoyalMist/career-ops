**Sell senior without lying**

- "I bring operator-side AI transformation experience: executive adoption, roadmap ownership, architecture, governance, and production delivery."

**If they downlevel me**

Only accept a Manager title if compensation is fair, the mandate is client-facing and strategic, and there is an explicit 6-12 month path to Senior Manager/Director. Otherwise, this is a distraction from better-fit Director, Head, Field CTO and AI Transformation Lead roles.
