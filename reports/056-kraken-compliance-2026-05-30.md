## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Senior compliance AI systems architect / RegTech automation architect. The role has high ownership inside Compliance but no explicit people-management mandate.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong on AI automation, regulated systems, audit/control thinking and crypto custody. Weaker on hands-on AML/compliance-operations domain depth.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "My strongest fit is building safe automation in regulated environments: audit trails, access controls, human checkpoints, evidence and operational ownership."
- "I have not spent eight years as an AML operator. I have worked across bank audit, GDPR controls, identity access, regulated crypto custody and finance/compliance data flows."
- "For this role to work, I would partner tightly with AML/sanctions SMEs while owning the architecture, workflow automation, control integrity and production reliability."
- "The reason this is interesting is Kraken's combination of crypto-native infrastructure, AI automation and compliance pressure."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a compliance analyst automation-builder framing. Continue only if the role is senior platform ownership with direct influence on AI governance, compliance architecture, production workflows, leadership reporting and compensation in the upper band.
