## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected level:** Head/Director of internal technology and AI enablement, reporting/partnering with CPTO.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong fit. This is not a generic small-team IT role because the mandate explicitly owns AI transformation, internal AI engineering, governance, tooling strategy, build-vs-buy and company-wide adoption.

**Caveat:** The team is described as small and includes IT admins/helpdesk. This is acceptable only if the role has strategic authority over internal AI/productivity tooling and CPTO-level visibility, not if it becomes day-to-day SaaS administration.
