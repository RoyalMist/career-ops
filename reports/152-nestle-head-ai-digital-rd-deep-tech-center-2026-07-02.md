## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Director / Department Head, high-visibility AI and digital innovation leadership inside a global R&D organization.

**Candidate natural level:** Strong fit with the target lanes: CTO / VP Engineering / Director of Software Engineering / AI Transformation Lead / Enterprise Architecture ([config/profile.yml](/Users/prism/Work/career-ops/config/profile.yml:15)).

**Strategy:** Apply only if the Orbe cadence is acceptable or likely negotiable. This is not a generic AI manager job; it is a department-head role with strategy, portfolio, executive influence, ecosystem building and team leadership. The pitch should be "AI transformation operator with production and leadership proof," not "food-science AI researcher."

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have led technology organizations of 40-50 people, owned roadmap and budget, and delivered AI adoption and production platforms in regulated environments."
- "My strength is turning AI and digital technologies into roadmaps, governance, operating models and deployed systems."
- "I would bring the leadership, architecture and execution layer, while partnering with R&D specialists on process modelling, simulation and domain science."

**If they downlevel**

Do not accept a role that is mostly portfolio coordination without decision rights. Accept only if:
- title and authority are truly Department Head;
- reporting line gives access to senior R&D leadership;
- team and budget ownership are explicit;
- hybrid cadence from Geneva is workable;
- total compensation is close to executive target or justified by scope and stability.
