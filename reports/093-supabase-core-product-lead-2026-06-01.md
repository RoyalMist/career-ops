## C) Level and Strategy

**Level detected:** Product leadership reporting to Head of Product.

**Candidate natural level:** CTO/CPO, Head of Engineering, Director. This is a credible senior-adjacent path if Supabase values operator/product leadership over pure career-PM pedigree.

**Sell senior without lying**

- "I have held CTO/CPO, Head of Engineering and Director roles where product strategy, architecture, execution and team development were inseparable."
- "I can translate engineering reality into product strategy, especially where platform reliability, enterprise readiness and developer experience collide."
- "I have managed managers and senior technical leaders; I would bring that coaching discipline to PMs while being transparent that my background is operator/product, not classic PM ladder."

**If they downlevel me**

Do not accept a pure IC PM downlevel. The role is interesting only if it remains product-leadership scope with founder/Head of Product exposure and compensation close to senior leadership expectations.
